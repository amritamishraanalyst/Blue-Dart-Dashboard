# Blue-Dart-Dashboard
1. Project Title / Headline
📦 Blue Dart Revenue Analytics Dashboard

A dynamic and interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyse Blue Dart’s revenue performance, order volume, average order value, cancellation rate, lost revenue, product-wise sales, category-wise contribution, state-wise revenue distribution, and quarterly revenue trends.

2. Short Description / Purpose

The Blue Dart Revenue Analytics Dashboard is an analytical Power BI report created to provide a clear overview of business performance using revenue and order-related data. The dashboard helps users understand which products, categories, and states contribute the most to revenue, while also identifying the financial impact of order cancellations.

This dashboard is useful for sales managers, revenue analysts, logistics teams, operations managers, and business decision-makers who need to monitor revenue performance, track cancellation losses, and make data-driven decisions regarding products, regions, and customer demand.

3. Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

• 📊 Power BI Desktop – Used as the main data visualisation and dashboard development platform.

• 📂 Power Query – Used for data cleaning, transformation, formatting, and preparing the dataset for analysis.

• 🧠 DAX Measures – Used to create calculated KPIs such as total revenue, average order value, cancellation rate, lost revenue, and total orders.

• 🧩 Data Modelling – Used to organise fields and support interaction between product, category, state, revenue, date, and cancellation data.

• 🎛️ Interactive Slicers – Used for filtering the dashboard by product category and purchase date range.

• 📁 File Format – The dashboard was developed in Power BI using .pbit format, with .png used for dashboard preview.

4. Data Source

Source: Kaggle dataset.

The dataset used for this dashboard was taken from Kaggle, an open-source data platform that provides datasets for analytics, business intelligence, and machine learning projects. The dataset contains Blue Dart-related sales and order information, including purchase date, product name, product category, customer state, revenue, order count, cancellation status, and revenue lost due to cancellations.

The dashboard analyses Blue Dart revenue performance for the period 01 January 2024 to 06 November 2024. The dataset includes product categories such as Laptop, Mobile, Headphones, Tablet, Accessory, Charger, Mouse, Speaker, and Cable. Before building the visuals, the data was cleaned and transformed in Power BI using Power Query.

5. Features / Highlights
Business Problem

Blue Dart needs to monitor its revenue performance and understand how sales differ across products, categories, and states. In raw data form, it is difficult to quickly identify which products generate the highest revenue, which regions are performing strongly, and how much revenue is being lost because of order cancellations.

The key business questions addressed by this dashboard include:

Which products generate the highest revenue?
Which product categories contribute most to total sales?
Which states are the strongest revenue-generating markets?
What is the total order volume?
What is the average order value?
How much revenue is lost due to cancellations?
How does revenue change across different quarters?

This dashboard solves the problem by converting raw order data into clear, interactive, and easy-to-understand business insights.

Goal of the Dashboard

The main goal of the dashboard is to provide a single-page revenue analysis tool that helps users:

Track overall revenue performance.
Monitor total orders and average order value.
Identify top-performing products and categories.
Analyse revenue by state.
Understand cancellation rate and lost revenue.
Track quarterly revenue movement.
Support better decisions in sales, inventory, marketing, logistics, and customer service.

6. Walkthrough of Key Visuals
Key KPI Cards – Left Panel

The left side of the dashboard presents five major business KPIs:

Revenue: ₹1.245bn
This shows the total revenue generated during the selected time period.

AOV: ₹112.846K
AOV stands for Average Order Value. It shows the average revenue generated per order.

Cancellation Rate: 0.30
This shows the cancellation rate. Since the value is displayed as 0.30, it can be understood as approximately 30% if the measure is formatted as a rate.

Lost Revenue – Cancellation: ₹525.420M
This shows the estimated revenue lost due to cancelled orders. It is an important metric because cancellations directly reduce potential revenue.

Orders: 15.69K
This shows the total number of orders recorded in the selected period.

Category Filter Panel

The category filter at the top allows users to analyse the dashboard by different product categories, including Accessory, Cable, Charger, Headphones, Laptop, Mobile, Mouse, Speaker, and Tablet.

This makes the dashboard interactive because users can select a specific category and view related revenue, product performance, state revenue, cancellation rate, and quarterly trends.

Date and Time of Purchase Filter

The date slicer allows users to filter the dashboard based on the purchase period. In the dashboard, the selected date range is from 01 January 2024 to 06 November 2024.

This helps users analyse revenue trends for a particular period and compare performance across different time frames.

Cancellation Rate by Product Name

This bar chart compares the cancellation rate across different products, including MacBook Air, Apple Watch, Dell XPS 13, Samsung Galaxy, Realme Cable, Logitech Mouse, HP Pavilion, Bose Speaker, OnePlus 9, Sony Headphones, Mi Charger, and iPhone 13.

The chart helps identify which products have higher cancellation rates. Products with higher cancellation rates may require further investigation into issues such as delayed delivery, pricing concerns, stock availability, customer dissatisfaction, or product quality problems.

Revenue by State

The revenue by state chart shows how much revenue is generated from different Indian states. The dashboard indicates that Maharashtra and Gujarat are among the strongest revenue-generating states, followed by Rajasthan, West Bengal, Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, Delhi, and Telangana.

This visual helps the business understand regional sales performance. Strong-performing states can be targeted for continued growth, while lower-performing states may need better marketing, logistics support, or customer engagement strategies.

Revenue by Category

This chart shows the revenue contribution of each product category. Laptop is the highest revenue-generating category, followed by Mobile, Headphones, Tablet, Accessory, Charger, Mouse, Speaker, and Cable.

This shows that high-value electronics, especially laptops and mobiles, are the major revenue drivers for the business. Lower-value categories such as cables and accessories contribute less revenue but may still support overall sales volume and customer convenience.

Revenue by Product Name

This chart ranks products based on their revenue contribution. The top-performing products include:

MacBook Air – approximately ₹145M
OnePlus 9 – approximately ₹145M
Sony Headphones – approximately ₹140M
HP Pavilion – approximately ₹130M
Samsung Galaxy – approximately ₹130M

Products such as iPhone 13, Realme Cable, and other lower-value items show comparatively lower revenue contribution. This visual is useful for product-level performance analysis and can help with inventory planning, promotional campaigns, and product prioritisation.

Revenue by Year and Quarter

The line chart shows quarterly revenue performance for 2024. Revenue appears relatively stable from January 2024 to July 2024, but there is a clear decline by October 2024.

This decline may indicate seasonal demand changes, reduced order volume, stock issues, higher cancellations, or weaker customer demand in the later part of the year. Management can use this insight to investigate the reason behind the drop and take corrective action.

7. Business Impact & Insights
Revenue Monitoring

The dashboard provides a quick overview of total revenue, total orders, average order value, cancellation rate, and lost revenue. This helps management monitor business performance without manually reviewing raw data.

Product Strategy

Products such as MacBook Air, OnePlus 9, Sony Headphones, HP Pavilion, and Samsung Galaxy are strong revenue contributors. These products can be prioritised for stock planning, marketing campaigns, and sales promotions.

Category Performance

The dashboard shows that Laptop and Mobile categories dominate total revenue. This indicates that premium electronics are the strongest business drivers for Blue Dart’s revenue performance in this dataset.

Regional Decision-Making

States such as Maharashtra and Gujarat show high revenue contribution. These regions may be important markets for future business growth, logistics planning, delivery optimisation, and targeted promotional campaigns.

Cancellation Control

The dashboard highlights a significant amount of lost revenue due to cancellations. By tracking cancellation rate and lost revenue, the business can identify problem areas and improve delivery accuracy, customer satisfaction, stock availability, and order fulfilment processes.

Quarterly Revenue Analysis

The decline in revenue toward October 2024 is an important trend. It suggests that the business should investigate possible causes such as seasonal demand reduction, operational issues, cancellation increase, or product availability problems.
